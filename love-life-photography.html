HOW TO OVERCOME THE FEAR OF VIDEO

I just wanted to share these AMAZING tips that my mentor shared with me when I first started doing videos for my business.

Make a Habit to Do 1 Video a Day

Don't over complicate it. Make a video about ANYTHING interesting about your day. These videos are for you to see only.

You only Get One Shot

No re-do's! If you mess up you mess up. The purpose of this is to get comfortable making mistakes.

Have Fun!

Be yourself and smile. If you practice enough you'll be super comfortable in front of the camera.
